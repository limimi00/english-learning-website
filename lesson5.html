1. be going to 表未来

主语肯定句否定句
II am going to study abroad.I am not going to study abroad.
He/She/ItHe is going to buy a car.She is not going to buy a car.
You/We/TheyThey are going to visit Paris.They are not going to visit Paris.

2. 过去式疑问句与否定句

疑问句：Did + 主语 + 动词原形?
Did you finish your homework? (你完成作业了吗?)

否定句：主语 + did not + 动词原形
She did not go to school yesterday. (她昨天没去上学。)
