1. 规则动词三态变化

现在式过去式未来式例句
playplayedwill playI play tennis every Sunday.
studystudiedwill studyShe studied English last night.
cookcookedwill cookHe will cook dinner tomorrow.
watchwatchedwill watchWe watched a movie yesterday.

2. 不规则动词三态变化

现在式过去式未来式例句
gowentwill goI go to school by bus.
eatatewill eatThey ate sushi last week.
dodidwill doShe will do her homework tonight.
buyboughtwill buyHe bought a new phone yesterday.
comecamewill comeMy friend came to visit me.
taketookwill takeI took a taxi to the airport.

3. 各时态基本句型

现在式：I / You / We / They + 动词原形  |  He / She / It + 动词 + s/es
过去式：所有人称 + 动词过去式
未来式：所有人称 + will + 动词原形
